Of course. While a language model excels at predicting the next word in a sequence, an **AI agent** takes it a step further. An agent has:

  • **Goals:** A defined objective it's trying to achieve.
  • **Environment Perception:** It can perceive its environment and context.
  • **Actions:** It can take actions to influence that environment.

Think of it as the difference between a brilliant author (LLM) and a protagonist in a story who can make their own choices (Agent).
